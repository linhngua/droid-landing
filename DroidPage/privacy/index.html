AIQID Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AIQID ("we", "us") handles information when you use the AIQID app and related services (the "Service").

1. Summary

AIQID provides AI-powered features, including fortune-style face readings. Depending on how the app is configured, the Service may process photos you choose to provide (such as a selfie) to generate results.

2. Information we process

A) Content you provide

  • Photos or images you upload or capture in the app (for example, a selfie).
  • Text you type into the app (for example, prompts or feedback).

B) Device and app information

  • Basic technical data needed to run the Service, such as device model, OS version, app version, and diagnostics (for example, crash logs), if enabled.

C) Purchase information

  • If you subscribe, Apple processes payments. We may receive limited information from Apple such as subscription status (active/expired) to unlock features. We do not receive your full payment card details.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide the Service (for example, generate your reading, display results).
  • Maintain and improve performance, reliability, and security.
  • Prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Face photos and biometric data

AIQID does not attempt to identify who you are. If the app analyzes a photo, it is used only to generate the in-app result you requested.

We do not sell your photos.

5. Data retention

  • Photos: By default, we aim to process photos only as needed to generate your result.
    • Option 1 (recommended): Photos are processed and not stored on our servers after the result is produced (except transient caching during processing).
  • Generated results: We may store your generated reading on-device and/or on our servers depending on app features you enable.
  • Diagnostics: Retained for a limited period to investigate issues.

6. Sharing and third parties

We may share data with service providers that help us operate the Service, such as:

  • Cloud hosting, content delivery, and security services.
  • AI processing providers (if your photo/text is sent to a model to produce results).

These providers may process data on our behalf under contractual restrictions.

We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

7. International transfers

Your information may be processed in countries different from where you live. We take steps to protect data consistent with this policy.

8. Your choices and controls

  • You can choose not to provide photos; some features may not work without them.
  • You can delete app data by uninstalling the app or using any in-app delete controls (if provided).
  • If we store data on servers, you can request deletion by contacting us (see Contact).

9. Children

AIQID is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us to request deletion.

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect information. No system is 100% secure.

11. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the updated version on this page with a new effective date.

12. Contact

If you have questions or requests, contact:
