Privacy Policy

AIQID

Effective Date: 12 Feb 2026

AIQID provides conversational AI interactions through a virtual droid companion. We are committed to transparency and minimizing data collection.

1. Conversation Data and AI Processing

When you use AIQID, the text you enter in the app is transmitted securely to OpenAI’s GPT models for the sole purpose of generating AI responses.

This includes:

  • The message you type
  • Limited recent conversation context necessary to generate a coherent reply

We use OpenAI strictly as a data processor to provide AI response functionality. We do not authorize OpenAI to use your data for advertising purposes.

OpenAI’s privacy practices are available at:
https://openai.com/privacy

2. Local 7-Day Personalization Summary

To improve interaction quality, AIQID generates a limited summary of your recent usage (up to the past 7 days).

This summary:

  • Is generated and stored locally on your device
  • May include conversational preferences, general topics of interest, and response style preferences
  • Does not store full historical transcripts beyond what is needed for short-term context
  • Is automatically reduced or replaced as it exceeds the 7-day window
  • Is deleted when the app is removed

This local summary is used only to adjust tone and relevance in future responses.

3. How We Use Information

User data is used only to:

  • Generate AI responses
  • Maintain conversational continuity
  • Improve interaction quality within the app
  • Ensure service reliability

We do not:

  • Sell personal data
  • Share data for advertising
  • Create marketing profiles
  • Track users across other apps or services

4. Data Retention

Conversation data is processed to generate responses and is not retained longer than necessary to operate the service.

Older conversation details are summarized locally and raw conversation data beyond operational needs is not persistently stored by us.

5. User Consent

By submitting messages within AIQID, you consent to the transmission of your messages to OpenAI for response generation.

If you do not agree, please do not use the conversational features of the app.

6. Security

We use encryption in transit and reasonable safeguards to protect data. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Users should avoid submitting highly sensitive personal information.

7. Children’s Privacy

AIQID is not directed to children under 13.
