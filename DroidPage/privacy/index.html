Privacy Policy

AIQID

Effective Date: 12 Feb 2026

AIQID is designed to provide conversational AI interactions through a virtual droid companion. We are committed to protecting your privacy and minimizing data collection.

Information We Process

When you use AIQID, your messages are processed to generate AI responses. This may involve secure transmission to AI service providers for response generation.

AIQID may store limited interaction preferences locally on your device to improve conversational quality.

We may collect limited technical information (such as app version or anonymous usage data) to maintain performance and reliability.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to:

  • Generate AI responses
  • Improve interaction quality
  • Maintain app functionality and security

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Personalization data is stored locally on your device where applicable. Deleting the app removes locally stored data.

We do not retain personal data longer than necessary to operate the service.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information.

Information may be shared only:

  • With service providers required to operate the app
  • If required by law

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

Children

AIQID is not directed to children under 13
